Our Inspiration

The trauma of the ongoing pandemic and the utter helplessness in the face of it hit us home when we lost a friend, 32 year old Rishi Pathak who succumbed to Covid-19, last week. In his memory, we have started an initiative to help provide commuting services, free of charge.

Rishi was a kind soul who only brought joy to the lives he touched. We owe it to him and the countless others to do our utmost at this time in our lives
