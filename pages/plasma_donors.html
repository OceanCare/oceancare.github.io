Plasma Donor Services

Relying solely on contribution from its patrons and voluntary payments from its customers, the porter service has helped 100+ needy people since its inception in the first week of May. TOI featured the initiative in their e edition as well as their print issue

With your support, we plan to expand services under the ‘Ocean Care’ umbrella to Delhi, Mumbai, Jaipur, Patna, Kota (Rishi's hometown) and Bangalore soon. We need each other to get through this together. Help us help one another. Rishi was a kind soul who only brought joy to the lives he touched. We owe it to him and the countless others to do our utmost at this time in our lives

We are not limiting ourselves to planned cities and initiatives. Also constantly working on multiple initiatives with colleagues to extend all support across India.
